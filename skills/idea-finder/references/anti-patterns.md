# Anti-Pattern Filter

Eliminate any candidate matching these before scoring.

1. "AI assistant for X" — too generic, no workflow specificity
2. "Better dashboard" — no workflow integration, easy to copy
3. Platform-dependent feature — one platform update kills you
4. Consumer social — winner-take-all, millions of users needed
5. Heavily regulated markets — liability exposure without deep expertise
6. Race to zero — only differentiator is price
7. Requires changing behavior fundamentally — near-zero B2B adoption
8. Two-sided marketplace from scratch — must solve supply AND demand simultaneously
9. Enterprise-only TAM — 12–18 month sales cycle, $50K+ CAC
10. Incumbent feature add-on — no standalone value

Quick check: first customer can be small/mid-market, standalone value exists,
doesn't depend on one platform not acting.
