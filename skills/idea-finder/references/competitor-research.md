# Competitor Research Guide

Per competitor: pricing/tiers, target customer, core value prop, key gaps, review sentiment.

Where to find negative reviews:
- G2 (g2.com) — filter 1–3 stars, read Cons
- Capterra — similar, good for SMB
- Reddit — "[product] reddit" and "[product] alternative"
- Hacker News — site:news.ycombinator.com [product]
- YouTube — "[product] review"

TAM sanity check (30 min max):
1. Count buyers (industry reports, LinkedIn, association data)
2. Price the solution (use WTP signals from interviews)
3. buyers × ACV = rough TAM

<$100M TAM = lifestyle business territory, not venture.
