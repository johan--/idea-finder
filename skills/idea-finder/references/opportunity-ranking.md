# Opportunity Ranking Rubric

Score each candidate on 10 dimensions (1–5 each, max 50). Eliminate below 25.

1. Pain frequency (1=yearly, 3=weekly, 5=daily)
2. Emotional intensity (1=mild, 3=frustration, 5=fear/burnout)
3. Financial weight (1=<$100/mo, 3=$500–2K/mo, 5=>$2K/mo or catastrophic)
4. Workaround intensity (1=none, 3=personal workaround, 5=team-level/headcount)
5. Incumbent defensibility (1=well-solved, 3=partial, 5=structurally unserved)
6. Interview accessibility (1=no access, 3=warm intro, 5=can interview this week)
7. Distribution feasibility (1=no channel, 3=reachable, 5=active community)
8. MVP buildability (1=>6mo, 3=2–4mo, 5=<4 weeks)
9. Founder-market fit (1=no connection, 3=adjacent, 5=personal experience)
10. Moat potential (1=easily cloned, 3=switching cost, 5=network effects/data)

Score 40–50: pursue aggressively. 30–39: needs 3+ interviews. <25: eliminate.
