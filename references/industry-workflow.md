# Industry Research Guide

## What to research per industry

For every industry the founder mentions, find:

1. **The dominant workflow** — what is the core repeating job this person does
   every day or week?
2. **The tool stack** — what software do they pay for? What's missing from it?
3. **The workaround signals** — where are they using spreadsheets, email, or
   manual steps instead of software?
4. **The community complaints** — what do practitioners say on Reddit, LinkedIn,
   niche forums, or review sites?
5. **The incumbent gap** — what do existing tools not solve, and why?

## High-signal workaround patterns

| Pattern | What it signals |
|---------|----------------|
| "I track X in a Google Sheet" | No software solves this; the pain is real |
| "I copy-paste between [tool A] and [tool B]" | Integration gap |
| "I have a reminder on my calendar to..." | Automation opportunity |
| "We hired someone just to do X" | Pain expensive enough to staff around |
| "I use [consumer tool] for work because [work tool] can't do X" | Feature gap |
| "I send an email every [period] to chase..." | Recurring coordination cost |

## High-density industries for operational pain

- Property management (maintenance, vendors, compliance, rent)
- Recruiting coordination (scheduling, candidate comms, feedback loops)
- Healthcare admin (prior auth, referrals, scheduling, billing)
- Legal operations (contract review, matter tracking, billing)
- Construction (subcontractor management, compliance, project tracking)
- Accounting/bookkeeping (close, reconciliation, document collection)
- Restaurant/hospitality (scheduling, ordering, vendor management)
- Real estate (transaction coordination, document management)
- Field services (dispatch, compliance, route optimization)

## Web search queries to run per industry

Replace [industry] and [role] with specifics. Run all five per industry:

1. `[role] biggest frustrations workflow site:reddit.com`
2. `[industry] manual process workaround spreadsheet pain`
3. `[tool name] negative reviews complaints site:g2.com OR site:capterra.com`
4. `[role] time wasted survey statistics`
5. `[industry] software gap underserved small business`
