# Competitor Research Guide

## What to research per competitor

1. **Pricing and tiers** — cost? Is there a plan for small operators or only enterprise?
2. **Target customer** — who is the actual buyer? What size company?
3. **Core value proposition** — what job does it primarily do?
4. **Key gaps** — what does it explicitly NOT do or do poorly?
5. **Review sentiment** — what do users love and hate?

## Where to find negative reviews

- **G2** (g2.com) — search product, filter 1–3 stars, read "Cons" section
- **Capterra** — similar to G2, good for SMB software
- **Reddit** — search `[product name] reddit` and `[product name] alternative`
- **Hacker News** — `site:news.ycombinator.com [product name]`
- **Product Hunt** — comments on launch day surface early frustrations
- **YouTube** — `[product name] review` — video reviewers describe workflows

## TAM estimation (30 min max)

1. **Count the buyers:** How many companies/people fit the target persona?
2. **Price the solution:** Realistic annual contract based on WTP signals
3. **Gut-check:** buyers × ACV = rough TAM

If TAM < $100M, probably too small for venture but fine for a lifestyle business.
