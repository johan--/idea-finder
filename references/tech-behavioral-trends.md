# Technology and Behavioral Trends

## What became newly feasible (2024–2026)

- LLM PDF extraction: COIs, invoices, contracts — structured data from unstructured docs
- Classification and routing: triage, categorization, assignment at human accuracy
- Agentic workflows: multi-step automation across tool boundaries without custom code
- Voice-to-structured-data: field workers capturing info without typing
- Webhook-native SaaS: real-time triggers standard across most B2B tools
- Low-code calendar APIs: sophisticated scheduling logic in weeks not months

## Behavioral shifts

- Gen Z in operational roles: less tolerant of spreadsheet-as-system, vocal online
- Remote/hybrid: coordination that happened by walking over now requires software
- Freelance normalization: solo operators have $50–$500/mo tool budgets
- AI expectation gap: enterprise AI validated markets, created larger gaps below

## New problems from recent automation

Enterprise tools (GoodTime, NetVendor, Jones) validated markets and raised prices
— creating larger gaps at mid-market and small business level.
